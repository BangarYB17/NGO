Volunteering

We like to organize memorable events. We intend not only to raise funds, but we also want people to fully enjoy our events. We work hard to prepare and hold each event. If you are a person who likes to be immediately involved, then voluntary cooperation with us is an excellent choice! We will be glad to see you on board!

Partnership

We are always looking for new ways to interact with people and organizations. If you know someone who could benefit from a PAG NGO partnership, invite them to contact us and tell us about their great work! Awareness is a major step towards gaining more support.

Donations

Corporate sponsorship

Corporate sponsorship Our sponsors make our events or projects what they are. We couldn't do what we do without them! If you would like to sponsor one of our future projects, please contact us. We will inform you about all the details.
