Community Cleanliness Drive

Together we can make our city cleaner

About the Event

Our Community Cleanliness Drive is a collective initiative dedicated to creating a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environment for present and future generations. This program brings together volunteers, local citizens, community leaders, and socially responsible individuals to actively participate in the cleaning and restoration of public spaces such as parks, streets, and common areas.
